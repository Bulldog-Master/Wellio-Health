## Channel Strategy

### TikTok
- **Frequency:** 4-5 posts/week
- **Format:** 15-60 second videos
- **Hooks:** Privacy contrast, feature demos, reactions
- **Hashtags:** #wellness #privacy #healthtech #quantumsafe

### Instagram
- **Frequency:** 5-7 posts/week
- **Format:** Reels, carousels, stories
- **Focus:** Visual product demos, social proof
- **Stories:** Daily engagement, polls, Q&A

### LinkedIn
- **Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week
- **Focus:** Professional outreach, thought leadership
- **Content:** Whitepapers, case studies, industry insights

### Email
- **Welcome sequence:** 5 emails over 14 days
- **Newsletter:** Weekly
- **Promotional:** As needed
