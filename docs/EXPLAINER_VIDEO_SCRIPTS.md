## Explainer 1 — What is Wellio? (20 sec)

### Scene
Individual using the APP Platform on their phone.

### Voiceover
"Wellio is a Wellness APP Platform designed for individuals. Track your wellbeing privately, with optional support from trainers, coaches, or clinicians."

### Visual Notes
- Clean phone mockup
- Today screen with FWI score
- Fade to Care Team invite screen
- End on Wellio logo

- **Voice:** Calm, confident, neutral accent

- **Colors:** Deep navy backgrounds, violet/cyan accents
- **Animation:** Smooth, minimal motion design
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk headings, Inter body

### Format
- 1920 × 1080 (16:9)
- 30 fps
- MP4 / WebM delivery
