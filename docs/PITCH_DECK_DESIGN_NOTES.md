## Overall Design Direction

- **Style:** Modern, clean, tech-forward
- **Feeling:** Trustworthy, secure, innovative
- **Approach:** Data-driven with human warmth

---

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Deep Navy/Black | #0D0F14 | Primary background |
| Violet | #7C3AED | Primary accent, CTAs |
| Cyan | #22D3EE | Secondary accent, highlights |
| White | #FFFFFF | Text on dark backgrounds |
| Muted Gray | #6B7280 | Secondary text |

---

## Typography

- **Headings:** Sans serif, geometric (Inter, SF Pro, or Space Grotesk)
- **Body:** Clean, readable, high contrast
- **Numbers:** Bold, oversized for metrics
- **Style:** High contrast for maximum legibility

---

## Iconography

- Minimal line icons
- Rounded corners
- Consistent stroke weight
- Monochrome or accent-colored

## Slide-by-Slide Guidance

### Slide 1: Title
- Wellio logo centered
- Tagline: "AI-powered wellness, protected by privacy."
- Subtle gradient background (navy to violet)

### Slide 2: Problem
- Split screen: "Traditional Apps" vs "What Users Want"
- Icons showing data collection vs privacy
- Bold statistic about data breaches

### Slide 3: Solution
- Phone mockup showing Today screen
- FWI score prominently displayed
- Key features listed with icons

### Slide 4: Platform Architecture
- Clear diagram showing:
  - Individual (center, largest)
  - Optional professional extensions (smaller, connected)
- Visual boundary between raw data and derived insights

### Slide 5: FWI Explanation
- Large circle score graphic
- Trend arrow showing improvement
- Component breakdown (sleep, meals, movement, etc.)

### Slide 6: Security
- Abstract node graph representing cMixx
- Shield icons for encryption
- "Post-Quantum" badge prominent
- Zero-trust architecture diagram

### Slide 7: For Professionals
- Two columns: Trainers/Coaches | Clinicians
- What they CAN see vs CANNOT see
- Privacy boundary visualization

### Slide 8: Market Opportunity
- TAM/SAM/SOM circles
- Growth projections
- Bold numeric typography

### Slide 9: Business Model
- Revenue streams visualization
- Pricing tiers
- Unit economics

### Slide 10: Traction
- Key metrics in large, bold numbers
- Growth charts
- User testimonials

### Slide 11: Team
- Founder photos
- Brief credentials
- Advisory board

### Slide 12: Ask
- Investment amount
- Use of funds
- Contact information
- QR code to app/website

---

## Imagery Guidelines

- **App Screenshots:** Real UI, not mockups
- **Backgrounds:** Abstract, soft gradients
- **Patterns:** Neural-style subtle textures
- **Photos:** Diverse, active individuals (if used)
- **Avoid:** Stock photos, generic wellness imagery

---

## Animation Suggestions

- Subtle fade-ins for text
- FWI score count-up animation
- Node graph gentle movement for cMixx slide
- Clean slide transitions (no flashy effects)
