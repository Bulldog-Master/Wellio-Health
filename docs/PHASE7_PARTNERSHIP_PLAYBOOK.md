## GYM & FITNESS CENTER PARTNERSHIPS

### Partner Profile
- Boutique fitness studios
- CrossFit boxes
- Yoga studios
- Large gym chains (pilot programs)

### Value Proposition for Gyms
- **Extend coaching beyond the session** — Keep members engaged 24/7
- **Insights without invading privacy** — Trainers see trends, not logs
- **Easy client onboarding** — Invite code system
- **Differentiation** — Privacy-first wellness is a selling point
- **Retention** — Engaged members stay longer

### Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Members | Features | Price |
|------|---------|----------|-------|
| Starter | Up to 100 | Basic FWI access | Free |
| Growth | Up to 500 | Trainer dashboard | $199/mo |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Full integration | Custom |

### Implementation
1. Gym signs partnership agreement
2. Trainers receive Wellio Pro accounts
3. Members download Wellio (free)
4. Members optionally connect to trainers
5. Gym receives engagement reports (anonymized)
